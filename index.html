Climate Risk Prediction System

MSc Dissertation · Ongoing

Weather series from the UK Met Office and NOAA arrive with gaps — and point forecasts hide how wrong they can be. This dissertation builds an end-to-end pipeline: autoencoder-based imputation to reconstruct missing observations, ARIMA/SARIMA vs LSTM forecasting benchmarks, and Monte Carlo Dropout for honest confidence intervals around every prediction.
